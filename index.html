About

When I first told my friends and family I wanted to be a developer, they thought I was crazy. We all grew up thinking coding was hard and boring. It is this stigma that lead to comments like “More power to you, I couldn’t do that” or “I tried it in high school but it’s not for me”. If you want my opinion, they are the crazy ones because I love it! I always love that thrill I get when I solved puzzles, especially logic puzzles. Enrolling in the Associate’s program for web design technology at ITT Tech was when I truly discover coding. I really love the challenge that coding presents to me when working on a project. It’s like solving a puzzle and the satisfaction I get when I solve it is exhilarating.

My deductive reasoning and intuitive skills have lead me to be the head of my team in less than a year at my current job. I am a problem solver. I am a mind reader. Yes, I am not kidding. My job requires me to use the information provided to me and deduce the root of the issue. There are many times the information is minimal. What problem can I solve for you?

When I am not solving the mysteries of code, I am an adventurer, virtually and in real life: travel, video/tabletop games, hiking, food, and photography.
